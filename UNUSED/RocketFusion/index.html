RocketFusion

Easy to use CMS for small to large companies or indivituals looking to make their online space easy

We created the RocketFusion platform not to compete with with Weebly, Wix or other click-n-drag corporate Content Management Systems, but as a Module-based alternative to WordPress, which many of our customers found just too confusing.

Small businesses don't have the staffing to spare to learn something complicated. And in either case, editing your website should be a simple affair. Just click, edit, save. It should be that easy. And with RocketFusion, it is.

Module-Based

RocketFusion is based on a Module-Based Workflow, with integrated Modules designed to accomplish specific tasks, such as Calendars, News, Photo Gallery, Online Forms, Dynamic File Areas, Frequently Asked Questions, Business Listings, the Contact Manager, and, for Towns and Cities, an Open Government Module that handles boards, their members, and their meeting agendas and minutes.

Each Module works a specific way, and is embedded and contained within the RocketFusion platform. A single sign-in gives you access to all Modules, and all Modules can be accessed either directly from the main website navigation, or attached to individual pages by category or in their entirety.

Module-Based

And, on top of all that, some modules are linked to each other in intelligent manners, such as online forms being dynamically linked to calendar events; dynamic forms, news and calendar items being linked to the contact manager for bulk emailing; photo galleries being linked to static pages for slideshows; PayPal being embedded in the photo gallery (simple shopping cart), calendar events (to pay for tickets) and online forms (to pay for class registration).

Almost all features have been requested by customers over the ten years we;ve been working on the software, and more will continue be added over the years... most of the time for free!
